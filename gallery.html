Hooyo's Specialities

Maraq Digaag

This warm soup with a domineering taste of chicken, tomatoes, potatoes, and carrots is a blessing to have. The tickling flavors of coriander and ginger act as the taste enhancer of this appetizer. Try this with bread and get mesmerized like a foodie.

Canjeero

Fermented pan bread. Although it looks like an Ethiopian injera, it is quite different from that. Soft and smooth, flour being the main ingredient makes the dish a fulfilling item. Try out with beef stew for a balanced taste.

Iskudheh Karis

An aromatic combination of rice and meat. Taste at its best! This preparation with rich flavors of cilantro, coriander, pepper, and garlic has an amazing taste. It is equally fulfilling and sumptuous for you!

Kaluun Iyo Bariis

A fish sauce with an amalgamation of rice and spices. Highly delicious, apart from the fish, you will get a unique taste of eggplant, carrot, and tomatoes. So, what are you waiting for? Bon appetite!

Malawah

A traditional pancake resembling Indian paratha in shape, is considered as a staple in Somalia. The softness of malawah is the outcome of flour, margarine, and water. To enjoy the taste better, have this with tomato sauce and feel the bliss!

Sabaayad

A flatbread preparation popularly recognized as kimis. It is prepared either in round or square shapes. Who knew flour could taste so good! Warm and crispy, this is a healthy preparation to have with stew or tea. The outer layer is blessed with crispness while it is smooth inside.

Sambus

Fried snacks served on Ramadan and other traditional ceremonies. These snacks with a sumptuous filling of ground beef and spices will fill your mouth and heart at one stroke. Spicy, flavorful, and delicious, try out these while hot.

Surbiyaan Hilib Adhi

A rice preparation accompanied by lamb. Sometimes the locals try out this preparation with goat instead of lamb. If you are traveling to Somalia during their biggest festival Eid, this is a must in their platter.

Suugo Suqaar

Pasta cooked with beef and xawaash (Somali spices). Enjoy the taste of pasta like never before with tomatoes, beef cubes, green and black pepper. The sugar subdues the acidity of tomatoes and enhances the taste of pasta.
